Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in deep cleaning?

Our deep cleaning includes comprehensive scrubbing of all surfaces, full appliance cleaning, sanitizing hard-to-reach areas, bathroom and kitchen deep clean, carpet and upholstery cleaning, and thorough dusting and vacuuming throughout your property.

How much does deep cleaning cost?

Deep cleaning costs range from £200 for a studio flat to £500+ for 5 bedroom houses. Prices vary based on property size and specific requirements. Contact us for a custom quote.

How long does deep cleaning take?

Deep cleaning typically takes 4-8 hours depending on property size and condition. Studio flats take around 4-5 hours, while larger houses may take a full day.

Do you provide deep cleaning for end of tenancy?

Yes, we specialize in end of tenancy deep cleaning to help you get your deposit back. Our thorough service meets landlord and agency standards across Hampshire and Surrey.
