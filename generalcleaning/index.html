Frequently Asked Questions - General Cleaning Services

What is included in general cleaning?

Our general cleaning includes dusting all surfaces, vacuuming carpets and floors, mopping hard floors, kitchen cleaning (counters, sinks, appliances), bathroom cleaning and sanitization, emptying bins, and tidying common areas. We focus on maintaining your home's everyday cleanliness with consistent, reliable service.

How much does regular house cleaning cost?

General cleaning starts from £60 per visit for weekly contracts (less than 3 hours) and £80 for monthly contracts. Prices vary based on duration and frequency. Weekly contracts offer better rates than monthly contracts. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your home's size and needs.

What's the difference between weekly and monthly contracts?

Weekly contracts offer lower per-visit pricing (e.g., £60 vs £80 for under 3 hours) because of the regular commitment and consistency. Monthly contracts have slightly higher per-visit costs but offer flexibility for those who don't need weekly cleaning. Both options provide the same high-quality service.

Do you provide cleaning supplies and equipment?

Yes, we bring all necessary cleaning supplies and professional equipment at no extra cost. Our eco-friendly, pet-safe products are safe for your family. If you prefer us to use your own products (for allergies or specific preferences), just let us know and we're happy to accommodate.

Can I schedule fortnightly cleaning instead?

Absolutely! We offer daily, weekly, fortnightly, and monthly cleaning schedules to suit your needs. Fortnightly cleaning is a popular choice for those who want regular service without weekly commitment. Contact us to discuss custom scheduling options that work for you.
